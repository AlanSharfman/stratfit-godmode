---

STRATFIT BUILD PHASE CHECKLIST v1

PURPOSE  
Provide a clear execution roadmap for building the STRATFIT platform from current state to MVP completion.  
This checklist ensures focus, sequencing, and progress visibility.

---

PHASE 1 — FOUNDATIONS (Platform Integrity)

Goal: Ensure architecture, contracts, and baseline logic are stable.

Confirm navigation follows navigationContract.ts  
Confirm baseline persistence works correctly  
Confirm Objectives store reads and writes properly  
Confirm Position renders from baseline only  
Confirm simulation triggers only from Studio  
Confirm contracts folder reflects current architecture  

Completion Criteria:  
Core platform logic is stable and deterministic.

---

PHASE 2 — POSITION EXPERIENCE

Goal: Deliver a clear and compelling current-state visualization.

Refine terrain rendering clarity  
Validate markers and timeline behaviour  
Confirm diagnostics toggles work  
Ensure liquidity visualization behaves correctly  
Improve explanatory overlays  

Completion Criteria:  
User clearly understands current state and meaning of visualization.

---

PHASE 3 — OBJECTIVES EXPERIENCE

Goal: Capture strategic intent cleanly and intuitively.

Validate objective inputs and constraints  
Ensure priority modes behave correctly  
Confirm Objectives do not alter terrain  
Provide clear guidance text  

Completion Criteria:  
Objectives feel purposeful and easy to define.

---

PHASE 4 — STRATEGY STUDIO

Goal: Enable scenario creation and simulation interaction.

Validate lever interactions  
Confirm simulation triggers correctly  
Ensure engine state feedback appears  
Verify outputs stored correctly  

Completion Criteria:  
Users can explore scenarios with reliable feedback.

---

PHASE 5 — COMPARE EXPERIENCE

Goal: Provide clear scenario evaluation.

Validate scenario selection and toggling  
Confirm probability outputs display clearly  
Ensure tabular comparisons are readable  
Provide insight summaries  

Completion Criteria:  
Users can confidently compare strategic options.

---

PHASE 6 — RISK MODULE

Goal: Deliver meaningful risk interpretation.

Confirm exposure metrics render correctly  
Ensure risk explanations align with outputs  
Validate visualization clarity  

Completion Criteria:  
Risk insights feel grounded and useful.

---

PHASE 7 — VALUATION MODULE

Goal: Translate strategy into value implications.

Validate valuation calculations  
Ensure ranges display clearly  
Provide sensitivity explanations  

Completion Criteria:  
Users understand value impact of decisions.

---

PHASE 8 — STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT

Goal: Provide synthesized insight narrative.

Ensure AI interpretation aligns with behaviour spec  
Validate structured insight output  
Avoid duplication with Compare  

Completion Criteria:  
Assessment delivers strategic clarity.

---

PHASE 9 — ROADMAP

Goal: Convert insights into actionable steps.

Validate action generation logic  
Ensure time-based segmentation works  
Confirm linkage to scenario  

Completion Criteria:  
Users receive clear next steps.

---

PHASE 10 — UX POLISH

Goal: Elevate product to premium experience.

Refine navigation layout and hierarchy  
Improve typography and spacing  
Ensure consistent component styling  
Add loading and feedback states  

Completion Criteria:  
Platform feels cohesive and professional.

---

PHASE 11 — MVP STABILIZATION

Goal: Prepare for external demonstrations.

Fix bugs and edge cases  
Validate performance  
Confirm data integrity across modules  
Review user flows end-to-end  

Completion Criteria:  
Platform is stable and demo-ready.

---

PROGRESS TRACKING  

Each phase should be marked:

Not Started  
In Progress  
Complete  

---

EXECUTION RULE  

Only one phase should be actively developed at a time unless dependencies require overlap.

---

SUCCESS CRITERIA  

At completion, STRATFIT delivers:

Clear understanding  
Strategic exploration  
Insightful evaluation  
Actionable guidance
