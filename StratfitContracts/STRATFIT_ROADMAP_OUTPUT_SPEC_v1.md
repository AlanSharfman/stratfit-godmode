---

STRATFIT ROADMAP OUTPUT SPEC v1

PURPOSE  
Define what the final Roadmap module delivers and how it translates insights into actionable strategy.

The roadmap is the culmination of the STRATFIT journey.

---

ROADMAP ROLE  

Transform insights into prioritized actions  
Provide clear time-based guidance  
Align actions with selected scenario  

The roadmap is forward-looking and prescriptive.

---

INPUT SOURCES  

Baseline (current state)  
Selected scenario  
Objectives  
Simulation outputs  
Risk signals  

---

OUTPUT STRUCTURE  

The roadmap must present:

Strategic priorities  
Milestone actions  
Time horizons (e.g. near-term, mid-term, long-term)  
Expected impact indicators  

---

TIME SEGMENTATION  

Near-term  
Mid-term  
Long-term  

Each segment must include clear action themes.

---

ACTION CHARACTERISTICS  

Actions must be:

Clear  
Concise  
Outcome-oriented  
Non-technical  

Avoid vague recommendations.

---

VISUAL PRESENTATION  

Actions displayed in chronological sequence  
Grouped by strategic theme  
Linked to expected outcomes  

Clarity over density.

---

INTERACTION  

Users must be able to:

Review actions  
Understand rationale  
Trace actions back to insights  

Roadmap should feel like a natural conclusion.

---

NON-GOALS  

The roadmap does not:

Re-run simulations  
Introduce new metrics  
Duplicate Compare analysis  

It synthesizes â€” it does not calculate.

---

SUCCESS CRITERIA  

Users leave the platform knowing:

What to do  
Why it matters  
When to act
